# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### What is AI Logo Maker?

AI Logo Maker is an intelligent platform that uses artificial intelligence to generate unique, professional logos for businesses and brands. It leverages Google's Gemini AI to create custom designs based on your specifications.

### Is it free to use?

The application offers both free and premium tiers. Free users get limited logo generations per month, while premium users enjoy unlimited generations and additional features.

### Do I need design experience?

No! AI Logo Maker is designed for everyone, regardless of design experience. Simply describe your brand, choose a style, and let AI do the work.

Currently, AI Logo Maker is a web application optimized for all devices. Native mobile apps are planned for future releases.

## Logo Generation

### How does logo generation work?

1. **Input**: You provide brand name, description, and preferences
2. **AI Ideas**: Gemini AI generates 4-5 logo concepts
3. **Selection**: You choose your favorite concept
4. **Generation**: AI creates a detailed logo based on your choice
5. **Download**: Save your logo in high resolution

### How long does it take to generate a logo?

- **Idea Generation**: 5-10 seconds
- **Logo Creation**: 30-60 seconds

### Can I regenerate if I don't like the result?

Absolutely! You can regenerate as many times as you want (within your plan limits).

### What design styles are available?

- Modern Minimalist
- Mascot Character
- Sharp-Lined Geometric
- Vintage/Retro
- Abstract Art
- Typography-Based
- And more!

### Can I customize colors?

Yes! Choose from predefined color palettes or create your own custom combinations.

### What file formats can I download?

Currently: PNG (high resolution)
Coming soon: SVG, PDF, JPG

## Troubleshooting

### Logo generation failed. What should I do?

1. Check your internet connection
2. Refresh the page
3. Try again with different parameters

1. Clear browser cache
2. Disable ad blockers
3. Check firewall settings
4. Try a different browser

1. Verify your email address
2. Check if Caps Lock is on
3. Try password reset
4. Clear cookies and try again
5. Contact support if issue continues

### Payment failed?

1. Check card details
2. Ensure sufficient funds
3. Try a different payment method
4. Contact your bank
